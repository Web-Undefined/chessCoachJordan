About Jordan

I'm Jordan Ashton, a successful adult improver with a passion for chess and teaching. Recently, I reached a peak USCF rating of 1600 under my coach’s guidance.

I focus on making chess fun and engaging. For beginners, I help build a solid foundation in opening principles, tactics, endgames, and classic games. For novices, I offer a structured, enjoyable approach to decision-making, advanced tactics, and search techniques.

As President and founder of the Cranston Chess Club in Rhode Island, I’m dedicated to helping you improve and enjoy the process. Let’s unlock your potential.

*Learn more about Rhode Island chess at Ocean State Chess.
